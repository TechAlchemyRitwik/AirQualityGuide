Safety Tips for High AQI Days

1. Stay Indoors

Limit outdoor activities, especially during peak pollution hours. Stay inside where air can be filtered and controlled.

2. Wear a Mask

Use N95 or N99 masks when going outside to protect yourself from harmful pollutants and particulate matter.

3. Use Air Purifiers

Install air purifiers at home and workplace to reduce indoor pollution and improve air quality in enclosed spaces.
