AQI Levels Explained

Category AQI Range Health Implications
Good 0–50 Air quality is satisfactory.
Moderate 51–100 Acceptable air quality. Some pollutants may affect very few sensitive individuals.
Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups 101–150 May affect sensitive groups like children and the elderly.
Unhealthy 151–200 Everyone may begin to experience health effects.
Very Unhealthy 201–300 Health alert: everyone may experience serious health effects.
Hazardous 301–500 Emergency conditions. The entire population is more likely to be affected.
